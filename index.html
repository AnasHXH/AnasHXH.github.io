Featured Research Projects

Prompting Robotic Modalities (PRM)

A modular framework integrating Large Language Models (LLMs) with multimodal sensing (audio, vision, and actuator data). Designed for robotic control and highly effective for human action recognition in healthcare. Emphasizes compact prompt-to-action pipelines and real-time human-AI collaboration.

Published in Future Generation Computer Systems (Q1)
Diffusion & Self-Supervised Models for Generative Perception

Efficient architectures for medical image enhancement, lesion detection, and cancer diagnosis utilizing diffusion modeling and transformer architectures under limited clinical data.

  • DM-AHR: Self-supervised conditional diffusion model for generating hairless dermoscopic images to enhance skin lesion visibility (Published in Cancers Q1).
  • TESR: Two-stage diffusion-enhanced architecture for super-resolution of remote sensing imagery under limited training samples (Published in Remote Sensing Q1).
  • CLEAR-Net: Cascaded transformer model combining local and global attention for image dehazing in adverse environments (Published in IEEE JSTARS Q1).
  • DiffPlate: Compact diffusion-based super-resolution model for low-quality license plates optimized for mobile/edge deployment.
